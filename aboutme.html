About Me

My name is Ngo Quoc Huy, and I am currently a junior mechatronics engineering student at Fulbright University Vietnam.

It has been 6+ years since my first engineering project when I was in high school, after that, engaging in different multi-disciplinary engineering projects helped me gain experience in addressing both theoretical and practical challenges. Embracing a growth mindset, I am proactively taking courses and doing personal projects to enhance both my engineering skills and personal development.

Even though I am eager to pursue the knowledge in every aspect of engineering, my interests mainly focus on mechanical design, embedded systems, and robotics. I perceive these areas as the skeletal muscles, nervous systems, and brain, respectively, forming the essential components of any complete machine.

For my working style, I like to do everything organizationally and punctually regardless of it is a individual or group task. Breaking down larger tasks into smaller manageable ones and tracking progress using Grantt chart is go-to approach whenever embarking on a new project.
